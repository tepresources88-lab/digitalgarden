## Short Summary
Every trader will appreciate this reliable, realistic, and systematic approach to trading financial and commodity markets. In a step-by-step manner, the author applies a rigorous mathematical discipline to financial speculation and explains how to analyze markets, forecast price movements, develop trading strategies, and manage trading capital.
